About

I believe in ego-less research. I don't think I'm any smarter than anyone else. My goal when researching is to discover the truth, not confirm it. I believe in equality between people. Logic before emotions and emotions before logic.

I don't mind messiness or imperfection. As Angela Davis once said, when speaking about the world's complexity, "The social world always exceeds our ability to fully comprehend it."

A few years ago, I traded in perfect-ion-ism for perfect-enough-ism. By no longer striving for perfection, I find peace in my work, accomplish more, and stress less.

I enjoy working with people who like sharing ideas and opinions. I don't believe in things designed by one person. If you didn't get help, you didn't design.

Being a UX researcher doesn't define me, but it does inspire me. Everyday I try to get a little better, a littler wiser, a little more empathic, and a little more grateful.

When you're ready to connect, send something my way at liz.alma.brown@gmail.com.
